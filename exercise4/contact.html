Find Us

Blues Café is located in the heart of Kathmandu, where the city’s noise fades into a gentle, calm atmosphere. Our café is designed to be a quiet escape — perfect for reading, studying, or just relaxing.

We are known for our soft jazz music, blue-toned interior, and cozy seating. Every corner is designed to feel peaceful, allowing you to slow down and enjoy your coffee without rushing.

You can visit us anytime during opening hours, or call ahead to reserve a table. We also host small creative meetups and book clubs.
